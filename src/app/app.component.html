About Michiana Rugby Football

The Michiaia RFC was established in 1972 as the South Bend Rugby Football Club. In July of 2003 the name changed to Michiana RFC due to the diverse players coming from the Michiana area.
After moving from pitch to pitch in the past, the Michiana RFC found a new home at the Elkhart County Moose Lodge. Over the past few years we have had the pleasure of playing on 3 of the most beautiful rugby fields in the Midwest. Our thanks go out to all those responsible, especially the Local Elkhart Moose Lodge #599, for providing us with a beautiful home. We are currently a division 3 team in the Northern Indiana bracket of USA Rugby.
